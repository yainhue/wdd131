GameNexus

Site Purpose

A centralized gaming platform where users can buy, save and look for all kinds of video games. this website will feature a home page, a store, and a profile page.

Scenarios

- What are the most popular games in the market right now? What's their price?

- I am excited for X upcoming game and I want to pre-purchase it

- I want to contact other gamers and chat with them
